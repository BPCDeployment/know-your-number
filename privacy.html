Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: 2026

Know Your Number is a free, static, educational tool. This page explains what data is (and isn't) collected when you use it.

The calculator itself

The calculator runs entirely in your browser. Your slider inputs and results are computed on your device. If you use the "copy link" or "share" feature, your chosen values are encoded directly into the URL itself (visible in your address bar) so the link can recreate your scenario for whoever opens it.

Anonymous visit count (everyone)

For every visit, we record a single anonymous entry on our own servers so we can count how many people use the tool and show a live map of roughly where visitors are. This entry contains no persistent identifier and is not linked across your visits or to your identity. It includes:

  • That a page was viewed, and which page.
  • Coarse location (country, region, and city) derived from your network connection. We do not store your full IP address.
  • Device basics: device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), browser, and operating system.
  • The site you arrived from, if any (the referrer).

Because there is no stored identifier, this cannot follow you around or build a profile. It does not include anything you type into the calculator.

Detailed usage logging (only if you agree)

The first time you visit, we ask whether you're OK with more detailed, privacy-friendly usage logging. Only if you choose "OK" do we additionally record how you use the tool, including the values you set in the calculator (for example product type and slider positions), presets, toggles, shares, and downloads, grouped under a random identifier stored in your browser so repeat visits can be counted together. That identifier is random. It is not your name, email, or account, and we do not try to work out who you are. If you choose "No thanks," none of this detailed logging happens (the anonymous visit count above still applies).

We do not sell any of this data, and we do not share it with data brokers. It is used only by us, to run and improve this tool, and may inform aggregate, anonymized reporting.

Your choice, and how to change it: the detailed logging only runs after you select "OK." To withdraw consent later, clear this site's data in your browser settings, and you will be asked again, or simply choose "No thanks."

Cookies & third-party advertising

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and manage your ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com. You can also opt out of many third-party advertising cookies industry-wide at optout.aboutads.info. This advertising is separate from the first-party analytics described above.

Age & targeted advertising

This site is intended for a general audience including people under 18. Ads shown here are configured to exclude alcohol, tobacco, and other age-restricted product categories at the ad-network level.
